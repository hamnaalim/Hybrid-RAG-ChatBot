Acme Corp Employee Handbook
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Annual Leave Policy

Employees receive 20 annual leave days each calendar year. Leave is accrued monthly
and must be approved by the employee's direct manager.

Unused annual leave may be carried forward. Employees may carry forward up to 10
unused annual leave days into the next calendar year. Any unused leave above the
10-day carry-forward limit is forfeited at year end unless an exception is approved
in writing by Human Resources.

2. Sick Leave

Employees receive 10 paid sick leave days each year. Sick leave does not carry
forward. Employees must notify their manager as soon as practicable when taking
sick leave.

3. Resignation and Notice Period

Employees must provide at least 30 days written notice before resignation. During
the notice period, employees are expected to complete a handover of ongoing work
and return all company property.

4. Remote Work

Eligible employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.
Remote work eligibility depends on role requirements and team coverage needs.
Core collaboration hours are 10:00 to 16:00 local time.

5. Parental Leave

Primary caregivers are eligible for 16 weeks of paid parental leave. Secondary
caregivers are eligible for 4 weeks of paid parental leave. Parental leave must
begin within 12 months of the child's birth or adoption.

6. Code of Conduct

Employees must treat colleagues with respect, protect confidential information,
and avoid conflicts of interest. Violations may result in disciplinary action up
to and including termination.
